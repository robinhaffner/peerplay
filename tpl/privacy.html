Collection of Personal Information From Site Users
Visitors to PeerDirect Publishing can access and browse the site, without disclosing any personally identifiable information. PeerDirect Publishing does track some information provided by your browser, such as the time and date of access, the content viewed or otherwise accessed on the site, the Web site you came from, the type of browser used, and other information that does not personally identify you. PeerDirect Publishing will not collect personally identifiable information without consent and will not share this data with any external partners.

PeerDirect Publishing may request information from its visitors or users such as feedback from evaluation questionnaires in order to improve or enhance its current services or develop new ones.

Use of Cookies
Cookies are a Web technology used by Web sites to identify a visitor as he/she navigates through the Web site. We use cookies to personalize our Web site and to track activity across all our Web sites. Cookie technology helps PeerDirect Publishing determine which features and services are important to which audiences so that we may know where to invest our ongoing development and creative efforts.

PeerDirect Publishing may place Internet "cookies" on users' hard drives. Internet cookies save data about individual users, such as name, password, username, screen preferences, and the pages of the site viewed. When a user revisits the site, PeerDirect Publishing may recognize the user by the Internet cookie and customize the experience accordingly. PeerDirect Publishing does not, and will not, use cookies or any other technology to track or report on member activity when they are not on our Web sites, nor pass member data to third party Web sites. You may decline Internet cookies by adjusting your Web browser settings. However, declining to use cookies may impede the ability of the site to function properly.

Typically, only aggregated data will be provided to commercial supporters of PeerDirect Publishing, and only such information that is relevant to their interests and/or programs that they support will be provided. However, to the extent that a commercial supporter of the program may already have personally identifiable information about a user, PeerDirect Publishing may inform that commercial supporter as to whether that user did or did not participate in any of its sponsored programs as well as information collected as a result of user participation in its sponsored programs. This information may include the time and date of access, the content viewed or otherwise accessed on the site, the Web site you came from, the type of browser used, and other information related to and provided by user participation in its sponsored programs.

Internal PeerDirect Publishing editorial and customer support staff have access to files containing personal information, including evaluation forms and aggregated participant information. These files can be accessed in order to respond to user questions or comments. Our staff will only use aggregate information and evaluation data in assessing educational needs and planning marketing activities. Personally identifiable information will not be used for this purpose.
